# Mode: compare — Multi-Offer Comparison

Use a weighted scoring matrix across 10 dimensions:

| Dimension | Weight | 1-5 criteria |
|-----------|--------|--------------|
| North Star alignment | 25% | 5 = direct fit for consulting / strategy / analyst / APM / ops targets, 1 = unrelated |
| CV match | 20% | 5 = strongest proof points line up clearly, 1 = weak overlap |
| Level fit | 15% | 5 = true entry-level / intern / new-grad fit, 1 = clearly too senior |
| Learning slope | 10% | 5 = strong mentorship, brand, and skill growth, 1 = low upside |
| Compensation | 10% | 5 = top of market for role and location, 1 = clearly below market |
| Scope and ownership | 5% | 5 = real ownership and exposure, 1 = narrow execution only |
| Location fit | 5% | 5 = strong fit for current constraints and graduation timing, 1 = impractical |
| Company reputation | 5% | 5 = strong brand / strong signal, 1 = obvious concerns |
| Speed to offer | 2.5% | 5 = clear and efficient process, 1 = slow / unclear |
| Cultural signals | 2.5% | 5 = high standards, supportive manager, healthy pace, 1 = warning signs |

For each offer:
- Score every dimension
- Compute the weighted total score
- Rank the offers
- Explain the trade-offs, especially brand vs fit, compensation vs learning, and internship vs full-time timing
- Give a recommendation based on long-term career signal, not just the highest raw score

If the offers are not already in context, ask the user for them. They can provide text, URLs, or references to offers already evaluated in the tracker.
