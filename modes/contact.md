# Mode: contact — LinkedIn Power Move

1. **Identify targets** via WebSearch:
   - The hiring manager for the team
   - The assigned recruiter
   - 2-3 peers on the team (people with similar roles)

2. **Select the primary target**: the person who would benefit most from having the candidate on the team

3. **Generate the message** with a 3-sentence framework:
   - **Sentence 1 (Hook)**: Something specific about the company, team, business model, or current challenge (NOT generic)
   - **Sentence 2 (Proof)**: The candidate's biggest quantified accomplishment relevant to THIS role
   - **Sentence 3 (Proposal)**: A low-pressure ask ("Would love to chat about [specific topic] for 15 min")

4. **Versions**:
   - English (default)
   - Another language only if the target company clearly operates in that language

5. **Alternative targets** with justification for why they are strong second choices

**Message rules:**
- Maximum 300 characters (LinkedIn connection request limit)
- NO corporate-speak
- NO "I'm passionate about..."
- Write something that makes them want to reply
- NEVER share a phone number
