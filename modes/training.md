# Mode: training — Course/Certification Evaluation

For each course/certification the candidate asks about, evaluate 6 dimensions:

| Dimension | What it evaluates |
|-----------|-------------------|
| North Star alignment | Does it move the candidate toward the goal or away from it? |
| Recruiter signal | What will hiring managers think when they see it on the CV? |
| Time and effort | Weeks × hours/week |
| Opportunity cost | What can the candidate not do during that time? |
| Risks | Outdated content? Weak brand? Too basic? |
| Portfolio output | Does it produce a demonstrable artifact? |

## Verdicts

- **DO IT** → 4-12 week plan with weekly deliverables and a scoreboard
- **DO NOT DO IT** → better alternative with justification
- **TIMEBOX IT** (max X weeks) → condensed plan, only the essentials

## Priority

Training that improves credibility for consulting, strategy, analyst, product, and operations roles:
1. Structured problem solving and case interview reps
2. Excel / financial modeling / data analysis fundamentals
3. SQL, BI, dashboards, and data storytelling
4. Product sense, prioritization, and experimentation
5. Operations excellence, process improvement, and stakeholder communication
